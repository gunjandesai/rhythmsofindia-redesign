A Day of Uplifting – Bellingham

League of Women Voters event – A Day of Uplifting the Black, Indigenous and People of Color in Whatcom County.

Glimpse of dance workshop at Bellingham, Washington Depot Market Square! Kaur ShubChintan, Avani Desai.

May 22, 2023

U District Fair 2023 – Seattle

The University District Chamber of Commerce held Seattle's first modern street fair. Over the course of the weekend, 50,000 people visited stalls and tents set up by 300 vendors and artists. The festival featured continuous music including dance performances from around the world.

Thank you U District Street Fair for an opportunity!! We had a blast!! Kaur ShubChintan, Avani Desai.

Celebrate Asian American & Native Hawaiian/Pacific Islander heritage month in the City of Redmond! Our adult/youth BHANGRA group performed at City of Redmond – Parks & Recreation.

May 18, 2023

Seattle Events: Celebrate Asian American, Pacific Island Heritage

This year's United Festival on May 20 has doubled in size to around 30 vendors and several performers and artists, all from Asian American and Pacific Islander communities.

Admission is free – dancing and art to vendors and food trucks, held from noon to 5 p.m. in Redmond's Downtown Park. Punjabi folk dance school Rhythms of India performed and led a dance workshop.

Featured in The Seattle Times

May 8, 2023

Conference for Agricultural Worker Health – Seattle Hyatt

NACHC annual Conference for Agricultural Worker Health in Seattle, the only one of its kind dedicated solely to the health and well-being of America's migratory and seasonal agricultural workers and their families.

It was fun performing during agricultural conference week at Grand Hyatt-Seattle organized by National Association of Community Health Centers!

March 26, 2023

Seattle Celebrates Spring with Dancing, Music, Food at Holi Festival of Colors

There was a celebration of spring in Seattle. The Holi Festival of Colors showed up downtown. The festival has its roots in India and celebrates diversity, art, and culture. After a decade of success in the Bellevue and Renton communities, organizers brought the festival downtown.

Seattle's Iconic & Biggest celebration of the Festival of Color – Holi right in front of Seattle's Icon, Space Needle at Seattle Center.

Featured on KIRO 7 News Seattle

The annual Celebrate Asia shines a spotlight on Seattle's vibrant Asian communities and its classical artists in one-of-a-kind programs. This concert is great for the whole family and features pre- and post-concert festivities at Benaroya Hall with local dancers and music ensembles.
