About Rhythms of India

Rhythms of India is the premier Bhangra dance school in the Seattle and Puget Sound area, founded by Shub C. Kaur. We offer authentic Punjabi folk dance classes for children and adults in Redmond, WA and Lynden, WA. Our mission is to share the joy of Bhangra — a vibrant, high-energy dance rooted in the rich cultural heritage of Punjab, India.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced dancer, our classes build confidence, fitness, and a deep appreciation for Indian cultural traditions. With over 200+ enrolled students and performances at major festivals and cultural events across Washington State, Rhythms of India is the largest Bhangra community in the Pacific Northwest.

Children & Adults Classes (13 and Older)

Bhangra dance is a vibrant and energetic folk dance from Punjab, India. It's characterized by fast-paced movements, lively music, and colorful outfits. As children grow older, they can progress to advance Bhangra dance classes that challenge them to perfect their technique, learn more complex moves, and perform at a higher level.

Advance Bhangra dance classes for kids aged 13 and older offer many benefits, including Technical Mastery, Artistic Expression, Performance Opportunities, Cultural Enrichment and Physical Fitness.

When enrolling your child in advance Bhangra dance classes, here are some things to look for: Experienced Instructors, Appropriate Level and Performance Opportunities.

Technical Mastery

Advance Bhangra dance classes focus on perfecting technique and mastering complex moves. As children practice more advanced steps, they improve their coordination, balance, and flexibility.

Artistic Expression

Bhangra dance is all about expressing yourself through movement. In advance classes, children learn how to incorporate their own personal style into the dance and use facial expressions and body language to convey emotions.

Performance Opportunities

Advance Bhangra dance classes provide children with opportunities to perform at a higher level. They can showcase their skills at competitions, recitals, and other events.

Cultural Enrichment

Bhangra dance is a significant part of Punjabi culture. Advance classes help children deepen their understanding of Indian culture and the history and traditions of Punjab.

Physical Fitness

Advance Bhangra dance classes are an excellent form of exercise that can improve cardiovascular health, build endurance, and help children maintain a healthy weight.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bhangra dance?

Bhangra is a vibrant and energetic folk dance originating from the Punjab region of India. It features high-energy movements, lively dhol music, and colorful traditional outfits. Bhangra is performed at celebrations, festivals, and cultural events worldwide and is a wonderful way to stay fit while connecting with Punjabi heritage.

Where are Bhangra classes held in Seattle and Puget Sound?

Rhythms of India holds Bhangra dance classes in Redmond, WA and Lynden, WA, serving the greater Seattle and Puget Sound area. Our spacious studio locations are easily accessible and offer a welcoming environment for dancers of all levels.

How much do Bhangra classes cost?

For information about class fees, please call us at (360) 527-5267 or (732) 331-7533, or visit our contact page to send us a message. We'll be happy to share pricing details and help you find the right class for you or your child.

Who teaches Bhangra at Rhythms of India?

Rhythms of India is led by Shub C. Kaur, an experienced Bhangra instructor with deep roots in Punjabi culture. The team also includes instructors Avani Desai and Bharat Kalra. Meet our full team on the instructors page.

What age groups can join Bhangra classes?

We offer classes for two age groups: Kids classes for ages 7–12 (Beginner Bhangra) and Teens & Adults classes for ages 13+ (Advanced Bhangra). No prior dance experience is required — everyone is welcome!

How do I register for Bhangra classes?

You can register by visiting our registration page and filling out the online form. You can also call us at (360) 527-5267 or email skg@rhythmsofindia.net for more information.

Do I need any prior dance experience?

No prior dance experience is needed! Our Beginner Bhangra classes are designed for students who are brand new to dance. Our instructors break down each move step by step, so you can learn at your own pace in a fun, supportive environment.

What should I wear to Bhangra class?

Wear comfortable workout clothing that allows free movement. Sneakers or dance shoes work well. For performances, traditional Punjabi outfits are provided. Bring a water bottle — Bhangra is a great cardio workout!

Do you offer performances and choreography for events?

Yes! Rhythms of India performs at festivals, cultural celebrations, corporate events, and private functions throughout the Seattle and Puget Sound area. Contact us to book a Bhangra performance or custom choreography for your event.

What are the benefits of Bhangra dance for kids?

Bhangra helps kids develop coordination, confidence, and cultural awareness. It improves cardiovascular fitness, builds teamwork skills through group choreography, and provides a fun, screen-free activity that connects children with Punjabi heritage and Indian traditions.
